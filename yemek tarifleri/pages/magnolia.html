Magnolia

Description

Essentially a sort of trifle, the Turkish magnolia pudding is a layered dessert consisting of a homemade custard, crushed biscuits, and fresh fruit. As you might expect, a strawberry magnolia uses strawberries as the fruit though other common fruits include bananas or apricots.

Ingredients

  • Granulated sugar
  • Egg yolks
  • Corn starch
  • All-purpose flour
  • Milk
  • Heavy cream
  • Vanilla extract
  • Fresh strawberries, sliced
  • Digestive biscuits

Steps

  1. Place the milk and vanilla in a soucepan. Bring to a boil. Keeping an eye on the milk.
  2. In another bowl whisk the cornstarch, granulated sugar, salt and egg until smooth.
  3. Slowly add the hot milk to the egg mixture whisking constantly. Pour the tempered egg mixture back to the soucepan.
  4. Place over medium heat and cook stirring constantly until the pudding comes to a boil and thickens.
  5. When mixture thickens take away from the heat and add the butter, stir until homogenized.
  6. Pour the pudding into a bowl and cover with a plastic wrap and let it cool to room temperature.
  7. In another bowl whisk the cream until thickens with a mixer. Pour through the room temperatured pudding and stir with a spatula until homogenized.
  8. In a serving cup put some biscuits, strawberries and pudding. Decorate it whatever you want.
  9. Refrigerate it a few hours to cool enough.
